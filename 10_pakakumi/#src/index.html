What is PakaKumi Game?

PakaKumi is an adrenaline-fueled crash game that has just become super hot in Kenya due to its fast-paced action. The principle of the game itself is pretty simple: bet on the direction the line graph of the multiplier will go and cash out before the graph crashes. The multiplier goes up as much as 3000X for a huge reward for those timing their exit correctly.

The betting range in PakaKumi is inclusive, with the minimum stake of KES 10 and the maximum stake at KES 3000. Great span makes it attractive to both the casual player and the high-stake gambler. The game is hosted on a platform that is rather easy to navigate, ensuring that even the most novice of players can get into the action quite fast.

Game Algorithm

The PakaKumi game operates on a transparent algorithm where the crash point is predetermined by a provably fair system. This means that each game's outcome is verifiable and cannot be manipulated, ensuring a fair gaming experience for all players.

To play, simply place your bet before the round begins, then decide when to cash out as the multiplier increases. If you cash out before the crash, you win your bet multiplied by the current multiplier. If the game crashes before you cash out, you lose your bet.

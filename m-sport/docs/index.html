Account Registration

You cannot start betting on sports without first making an account. The registration process is quite easy, so don't be too worried about it. Follow these steps to create your MSport account:

  1. Open the website. Once you do, you will see the Sign-Up button on the top right corner of the screen. Click it.
  2. A dialog box will appear asking you for your phone number. Provide your number and click Next.
  3. Now, you will need to create a password.
  4. A verification code will be sent to your phone number. Click on the link to verify your identity.

Once that's done, your account is all set! You can also find full registration instructions on this page.

Logging into Your Account

The MSport login process is similarly simple. This is what you need to do:

  1. Open the website. Beside Sign Up, there will be a Login button.
  2. Beside the Login button, there will be two fields: one for your mobile number and one for your password. Enter your number and password here.
  3. Click Login.

That's that. Logging into the bookmaker's website is as easy as pie.
